# UHC Challenge
# Minecraft Version: Fabric 26.2
**UHC Challenge** is a hardcore-focused Fabric mod designed to provide a balanced, challenging, and engaging Ultra Hardcore experience. This mod changes fundamental mechanics, rebalances Golden Apples, and adds a brand new set of custom advancements to conquer!

## Features

### 💀 Hardcore Exclusive
The mod's mechanics are **only** active when playing on a **Hardcore** world. If you play on standard Survival mode, the game functions exactly like Vanilla Minecraft.

### ❤️ Double Health & No Natural Regeneration
- **40 HP Base Health**: You spawn with 20 red hearts instead of the usual 10.
- **No Natural Regeneration**: The `naturalRegeneration` gamerule is permanently disabled. Eating food will **not** restore your health! The only way to heal is through potions, golden apples, or suspicious stews.

### 🛡️ The Grace Period
To help you get started, you receive a **10-minute Grace Period** upon joining the world:
- You are granted **Absorption IV** (8 extra golden hearts) for exactly 10 minutes.
- **No Free Refills**: If you take damage and lose these golden hearts, they will **not** regenerate. This acts as a one-time shield for the first phase of the game.
- After 10 minutes, the shield shatters completely and you receive a chat notification.

### 🍎 Golden Apple Rebalance
Because natural regeneration is disabled, Golden Apples are the primary source of healing. They have been rebalanced to suit the 20-heart health pool:

**Golden Apple:**
- **Regeneration I** (heals 4 hearts)
- **Absorption I** (2 golden hearts for 5 minutes)

**Enchanted Golden Apple:**
- **Craftable again!** The classic recipe is back: 1 Golden Apple surrounded by 8 Gold Blocks.
- **Regeneration II** (heals 8 hearts)
- **Absorption IV** (8 golden hearts for 5 minutes)
- **Resistance I** & **Fire Resistance** (for 5 minutes)

### 🏆 Custom Advancements
Test your skills with a brand new advancement tab specifically designed for UHC!

1. **UHC Challenge**: Survive the most balanced challenge! (Root)
2. **The Barrier is Broken**: Survive the first 10 minutes without losing any red hearts.
3. **Alchemy for the Poor**: Craft or obtain a Golden Apple.
4. **Forbidden Technology**: Obtain the legendary Enchanted Golden Apple.
5. **Peaceful Sleep**: Survive 3 nights without attempting to sleep.
6. **Gravity is Just a Suggestion**: Perform an MLG from at least 100 blocks.
7. **Brush with Death**: Drop to 5 hearts or fewer, then heal back to full.
8. **Flawless Defender**: Win a raid without losing a single health point.
9. **Dragon Slayer**: Defeat the Ender Dragon losing a maximum of 5 hearts in the End.

### 💬 Commands
- `/uhc` - Displays the welcome message containing the basic rules of the challenge.

## Installation

1. Download and install the [Fabric Loader](https://fabricmc.net/).
2. Drop the `uhc-challenge` `.jar` file into your `.minecraft/mods` folder.
3. (Required) Ensure you have the [Fabric API](https://modrinth.com/mod/fabric-api) installed.
4. Create a new world, set the mode to **Hardcore**, and enjoy the challenge!
